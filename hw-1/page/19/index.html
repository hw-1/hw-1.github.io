lipo 分离/合并 ARMV7、ARM64架构

2016-03-08
hw-1

###分离：

lipo -thin armv7 xxxx.dylib -output xxxx_v7.a
lipo -thin arm64  xxxx.dylib -output xxxx_64.a

###合并

lipo -create ./xxxx_v7.dylib ./xxxx_64.dylib -output ./xxxx.a
